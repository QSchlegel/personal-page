The **General Systems Vehicle** (GSV) is the largest class of Culture ship: less a craft than a mobile nation — kilometres in scale, home to millions or billions of people, capable of building almost anything, and entirely the expression of a single [[The Architecture of Minds|Mind]]'s character.

Smaller classes range from the all-purpose General Contact Unit, the Culture's everyday envoy, down through a discreet hierarchy of faster, sharper vessels whose names grow euphemistic in proportion to their armament. Power for all of it comes from [[The Energy Grid]]. See [[Ship Minds and Avatars]].
