Connection is its own kind of genius. The **bridge** is the span thrown between two disciplines held in one head at once — and it is a first-class skill, not the consolation prize handed to those who failed to specialise. The rare and valuable thing was never one field mastered to the bone; it is two fields carried together, and the structure that only becomes visible when you stand with a foot on each shore.

This is what living in [[The Seams|the seams]] is finally for. The seam tells you where the interesting problem hides; the bridge is the genius of crossing it — seeing the load-bearing insight that neither field, alone, could ever have seen. [[Depth]] supplies the shores worth joining, but the span is its own act of invention. Synthesis is not what you do when you cannot go deep; it is a thing you go deep *into*.
