**Contact** is the broad arm responsible for exploration and relations with other civilisations; **Special Circumstances** is its smaller, sharper division — the part that does the morally difficult, deniable work of intervention and espionage.

[[The Architecture of Minds|Minds]] are central to both: they plan, recruit, and manipulate, and carry much of the burden of deciding when a utopia is entitled to meddle in the affairs of those who never asked for its help. Much of the drama of the series lives in exactly that tension. See also [[The Culture]].
