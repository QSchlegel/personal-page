Breadth is not a free lunch, and a [[The Generalist Manifesto|manifesto]] that pretended otherwise would not be worth reading. So here is **the bill**, named plainly: you will never be the best on Earth at the one thing. That title belongs to [[The Specialist|the specialist]] who went all the way down, and it is theirs to keep.

This is a real cost, not a rhetorical flourish. You traded the gold medal in a single event for a ticket to all of them — and a ticket to all of them is not a podium in any of them. The arithmetic is honest only if you let it sting.

But the trade was made clear-eyed, and there is no resentment in it. We would make it again tomorrow. To pay the bill gladly is itself a kind of [[The Vow|vow]]: you do not get to want both prizes.
