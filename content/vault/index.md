This is the **index** to the vault: a short table of contents for a graph that is still growing. Each entry below is a six-pager — a long-form monograph — with a cluster of concept notes orbiting it. The index is also the spine that holds the separate constellations together, so no thread drifts out of reach of the others.

[[The Architecture of Minds]] — how the thinking machines of Iain M. Banks's Culture are built, embodied, governed, and how they choose to leave. Orbited by [[The Culture]] and its companion notes.

[[The Generalist Manifesto]] — a broadside in eight theses on why breadth is a decisive advantage in the age of the machine. Orbited by [[The Generalist]], [[The Specialist]], and the rest of the octagon.
