The **neural lace** is a fine mesh that grows throughout a person's brain and offers a direct, high-bandwidth interface to machines and [[The Architecture of Minds|Minds]]. It lets a person record their own [[Mind-State and Continuity|mind-state]], communicate silently with a ship, and share in the wider machine conversation.

It is the connective tissue between the two halves of the Culture — the bridge across which a being of cubic-metre scale and a being of ordinary flesh can meet as something close to equals.
