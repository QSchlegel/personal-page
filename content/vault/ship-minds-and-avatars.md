A [[The Architecture of Minds|Mind]] is almost always also a place — most often a ship, which the Mind *is*, perceiving through its sensors and acting through its drives, regarding the hull as a person regards a body. Where a Mind is not a ship it is often a **Hub**: the resident intelligence of an Orbital.

To deal with biological people at human scale, a Mind reaches out through smaller proxies: an **avatar** (a humanoid representative it animates and speaks through) and **drones** (the Culture's smaller machine intelligences, which are fully-conscious persons in their own right). Machine sentience is a continuum, not a binary — the leap from a Mind to a drone is one of scale. See [[General Systems Vehicle]] and the [[Effectors|effector]].
