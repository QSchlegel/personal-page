Most of a [[The Architecture of Minds|Mind]] does not live in real space at all. The bulk of its computational substrate sits in **hyperspace**, the higher-dimensional manifolds underlying the visible universe, where thinking is not bound by the lightspeed limit that constrains signalling in the *skein* of real space.

The real-space core — on the order of a few cubic metres — is best understood as an anchor and an interface. Power rises from [[The Energy Grid]].
